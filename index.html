Digital Fashion & Sustainability

Empowering students at UTA's FabLab to create with precision, minimize waste and innovate with denim.

Our Mission

At the University of Texas at Arlington FabLab, we bridge technology and fashion. By teaching students to draft patterns digitally, cut denim with lasers and assemble garments using advanced sewing techniques, we aim to reduce fabric waste and promote sustainable design.
